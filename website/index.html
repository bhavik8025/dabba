Trust, engineered

Money apps live or die on one question: can someone cheat?

  • Server-stamped attribution. "Added by", "edited by", "deleted by" are written by the database itself — not the app — so they can't be faked even with technical tricks.
  • Nothing disappears. Hard-deleting records is impossible by design. Deletes become visible, restorable tombstones; payments are immutable once recorded.
  • Every number is explainable. Tap any balance and see the exact entries behind it. No "trust me bro" maths anywhere.

Questions, answered

FAQ

Is Dabba really free?

Yes — free forever, for everyone in your group. No ads, no premium tier, no hidden costs. The app is deliberately engineered to run on free infrastructure, which is also why login uses a PIN instead of paid SMS OTPs.

How is Dabba different from Splitwise?

Splitwise is a general expense splitter. Dabba does one job: daily shared tiffin in PGs and flats. It understands tiffin prices, extra rotis, shared boxes split between specific people, and one-off extras — so logging a day takes 10 seconds instead of a form-filling session.

Do I need the Play Store or App Store?

No store needed. Dabba is an installable web app (PWA): open the link, tap "Add to Home Screen", and it behaves like a normal app with its own icon — on Android, iPhone and desktop.

Does signing up need an OTP or email?

No. You log in with your phone number and a 6-digit PIN you choose. No SMS, no email verification — that keeps it instant and keeps the app free.

Can someone secretly edit or delete an entry?

No. Every add, edit and delete is stamped with who did it — by the server, so it can't be faked. Deleted entries stay visible as restorable "deleted by X" tombstones, and payments can never be edited, only deleted with a visible trace.

How do shared tiffins get split?

Tick who shared, and Dabba divides the cost equally among exactly those people, accurate to the paisa — e.g. 2 tiffins worth ₹140 shared by 3 people is ₹46.67 each. Individual items and extras are tracked per person on top.

What happens at month end?

Open Balances for live who-pays-whom suggestions, or generate an audit report for any date range — per person: days, tiffins, rotis, ate, paid, net. One tap copies it as text for your WhatsApp group.

What if someone forgets their PIN?

The group admin can reset it from the database dashboard in about a minute — steps are in the README. PINs can't be reset by SMS or email because the app deliberately uses neither.
